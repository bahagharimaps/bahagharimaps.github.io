# Mt. Dialanese
<img src="maps/A3 f4 - Mt. Dialenese - 2022.12.14.png">
Trail map covers the area showing the Mt. Dialanese trail as mapped by Kiyaw Mountaineers and Schadow1 Expeditions.

Scale: 1:25,000
Datum: WGS84 UTM51N
